# Playbook: Privileged Account Change

1. Confirm the group, added member, actor, host, and change ticket.
2. Contact the system owner through an independent channel.
3. If unauthorized, remove membership and disable the added account.
4. Review actor and member authentication history.
5. Search for additional account, service, scheduled-task, and policy changes.
6. Reset credentials where compromise is suspected.
7. Record authorization status, duration of exposure, actions taken, and preventive controls.
